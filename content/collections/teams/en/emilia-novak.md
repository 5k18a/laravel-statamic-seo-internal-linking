  biography: |-
    Emilia Novak is a highly sought-after architect whose portfolio includes award-winning cultural centers, luxury residential towers, and innovative public spaces. Based in Prague, she brings a distinctly European sense of aesthetic balance to her designs, merging artistry with practical engineering. Educated in both architecture and environmental design, Emilia excels at crafting spaces that are as sustainable as they are visually striking. Her work reflects a strong respect for historical context, seamlessly integrating modern elements into established urban landscapes. Over her 18-year career, Emilia has collaborated with multidisciplinary teams to bring visionary concepts to life, ensuring every project delivers lasting impact. Her leadership in design workshops fosters creativity while maintaining adherence to technical and regulatory standards. Clients value her ability to translate abstract ideas into bold, functional realities that stand the test of time.

    Emilia maintains close contact with clients and team members through on-site visits, design review sessions, and virtual collaboration tools. She is fluent in Czech, English, and German, enabling her to bridge communication gaps in multinational projects. She keeps her professional calendar open for architectural consultations and project milestones. Beyond architecture, Emilia is passionate about urban gardening, fine arts, and mentoring young women pursuing careers in design and construction.

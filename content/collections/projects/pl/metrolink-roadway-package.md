          text: 'The MetroLink Roadway Package is a major infrastructure initiative aimed at improving urban mobility and connectivity. Covering 12.5 km of roadway, it links metro stations with key residential, business, and industrial hubs. The project incorporates durable road surfaces, smart traffic signaling, and efficient drainage systems to ensure long-term reliability. Dedicated bus and cycling lanes are included to encourage sustainable transportation. Construction was carried out with minimal disruption to existing traffic flow, using phased execution methods. Landscaping and sound barriers along the route reduce noise pollution while enhancing aesthetics. Safety features such as pedestrian overpasses, crossings, and proper lighting make the road accessible to all. By integrating smart transport planning with robust engineering, MetroLink boosts urban productivity and reduces daily travel times. It represents a forward-looking model of sustainable urban infrastructure.'

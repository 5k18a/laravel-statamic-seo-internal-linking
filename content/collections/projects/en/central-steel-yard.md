Covering 22,000 m², it features large-span storage areas, high-load bearing floors, and specialized handling facilities for steel and metal products. Wide access roads and docking bays ensure smooth movement of trucks and cargo. The facility includes cranes, automated material handling systems, and safety zones for efficient operations. Dedicated office blocks support administrative functions within the complex. Robust fire safety, surveillance, and emergency response systems guarantee safe working conditions. Sustainability is addressed with energy-efficient lighting and water recycling systems. Strategically located near transport corridors, the yard ensures fast supply chain integration. Central Steel Yard is built for durability, scalability, and long-term industrial performance.'
